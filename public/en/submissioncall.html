Call for Sessions

Do you want to propose a talk (between 30 and 45 minutes), a lightning talk (between 5 and 15 minutes), or a live demo of a software (between 1 and 2 hours)? The language used during the Libre Graphics Meeting is English, so your session should be done in this language.

Please fill this form to submit your session proposal: Session proposal for the LGM 2021 .

This form will be open from March 15th to April 19th. We will contact you a few days after the call is closed to inform you of our decision.

If your proposal is accepted, you will need to sent us a video recording of your session before the event (except for live demo sessions, for which it is optional). During the event, you will need to be online for the Q&A session after your video to answer the questions from participants.
