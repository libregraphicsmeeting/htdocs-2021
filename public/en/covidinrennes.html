health situation in Rennes

Since the beginning of the pandemic, the region of Brittany has been one of the least affected in France, and didn't reach any critical alert threshold. However, currently we have to respect the following rules:

  • Curfew at 18h00 (6:00 pm)
  • Mandatory mask in public spaces

We hope that the increase of vaccinated people will allow to alleviate those rules by the end of April, which would improve the conditions to organize the physical event at the dates of the LGM end of May. Of course we will keep everyone interested to attend informed of the evolution of the situation.
